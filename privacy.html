Privacy Policy for Coin Database App

Last updated: August 27, 2025

The Coin Database App is a local desktop application used to manage your personal coin collection. This application does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information or coin data with third parties.

Data Storage: All coin data, images, and metadata are stored locally on your computer. No external servers are contacted by this application.

Third-Party Services: If you choose to integrate with services such as eBay for market price lookup, only the minimum required information (e.g., coin type or grade) is sent to eBay through their official API. No personal data is included.

Cookies and Tracking: This application does not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking mechanisms.

Changes to this Privacy Policy: Any updates to this policy will be communicated in future versions of the application. Always ensure you are using the latest version for full compliance.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer at JosephAv-CoinCata@example.com.
